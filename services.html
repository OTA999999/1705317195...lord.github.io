Browse and Select: Visit the online pharmacy's website or app, browse through the catalog, and select the medicines you need.

Add to Cart:

Once you've selected your medicines, add them to your shopping cart.

Upload Prescription:

If your medication requires a prescription, you can easily upload a photo of your doctor's prescription.

Place Order:

Proceed to checkout, enter your delivery details, and choose your preferred payment method.

Delivery:

Sit back and relax while your order is processed and delivered right to your doorstep.
